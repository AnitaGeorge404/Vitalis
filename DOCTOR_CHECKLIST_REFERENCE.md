## Warning Signs That Need Escalation

- Breathing becomes harder or faster

- Severe pain develops
- Confusion or drowsiness increases

- Vomiting becomes persistent
- Bleeding increases
- Numbness or weakness spreads

## What to Tell Your Doctor

**Have ready:**

- When symptoms started
- How they've changed (better/worse/same)
- Vital signs if available (temperature, HR, BP)
- Any medications you're taking
- Allergies to medications
- Recent illnesses or exposures
- Symptom triggers (food, activity, position)
- What has/hasn't helped
