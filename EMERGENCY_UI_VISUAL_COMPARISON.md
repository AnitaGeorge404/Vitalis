## ğŸ¯ Key Design Decisions Explained

### 1. Why Sticky Bar at Top?
- **Always accessible**: No matter where user scrolls
- **Muscle memory**: Same location every time
- **Two critical actions**: Call 911 and Start CPR
- **High contrast**: Red background, white/dark buttons

### 2. Why 2Ã—2 Grid on Mobile?
- **Perfect balance**: Not too crowded, not too sparse
- **Thumb-friendly**: Easy to reach with one hand
- **Consistent spacing**: Prevents mis-taps
- **Scales to 4Ã—1 on desktop**: Natural responsive behavior

### 3. Why Icon + Label (No Descriptions)?
- **Faster scanning**: Brain processes images faster than text
- **Less cognitive load**: No reading required
- **Universal understanding**: Icons transcend language
- **Space efficiency**: More tools visible

### 4. Why Progressive Disclosure?
- **Prevent overwhelm**: Show most critical first
- **Reduce clutter**: Keep interface clean
- **Optional access**: Advanced tools available but not intrusive
- **Scalability**: Easy to add more tools later

### 5. Why High Contrast Red?
- **Urgency signal**: Red = emergency in all cultures
- **Attention grabbing**: Stands out immediately
- **Accessibility**: High contrast ratio (9.5:1)
- **Brand consistency**: Matches emergency theme
